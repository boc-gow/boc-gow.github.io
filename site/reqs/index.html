The minimum gold donations should be made by the end of day 1. Tasks should be finished in this order:

  1. Basic tasks: The order of the basics tasks doesn't matter. If there are any basic tasks left, pick whichever you want and donate to it.
  2. Epic tasks: If the basics are done, follow the pulsating light, the lens flare on the tasks page as it will indicate what task is current and should be completed next. Only donate to that task!
  3. Otherwise: Donate to the one available task :)

Inexperienced players are exempt from gold donations if they have not yet completed levelling kingdoms to 10. Levelling all kingdoms to 10 should be your highest priority during the early game. Members in this group are expected to make regular and consistent progress.
